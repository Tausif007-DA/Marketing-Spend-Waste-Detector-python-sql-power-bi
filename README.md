# 🚀 Marketing Spend Waste Detector

### End-to-End Data Analytics Project (Python | SQL | Power BI)

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

In many ecommerce companies, large marketing budgets are spent across multiple channels without clear visibility into **which campaigns are actually profitable**.

This project answers a critical business question:

> 💡 *“Where is the company wasting marketing money?”*

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify **loss-making campaigns**
* Detect **high spend, low return inefficiencies**
* Analyze **channel performance**
* Understand **customer drop-off behavior**
* Enable **data-driven budget allocation**

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* ❌ YouTube Ads showed **consistently low ROI**, indicating inefficient spend
* ✅ Facebook Ads emerged as the **highest ROI channel**
* 📉 Email campaigns had the **lowest CTR**, signaling poor engagement
* 💸 Multiple campaigns had **high spend but negative profit**
* ⚠️ Marketing spend is **not directly proportional to revenue**

## 🐍 Python Workflow

* Handled missing values & inconsistencies
* Recalculated key metrics (Profit, ROI)
* Created business flags:

  * `Negative_ROI`
  * `High_Spend_Low_Conversion`
  * `Zero_Clicks`
* Performed exploratory analysis:

  * ROI distribution
  * Spend vs Revenue relationship

---

## 🗃️ SQL Analysis

### 🔍 Channel Performance

* Identified best and worst performing marketing channels

### 🔍 Campaign Waste Detection

* Detected campaigns with:

  * High spend
  * Low revenue
  * Negative ROI

### 🔍 Funnel Insights

* Email → Low CTR
* YouTube Ads → Low conversion rate
